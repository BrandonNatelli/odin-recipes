Hot Water

A steaming cup of water begging to be enjoyed

Nothing is better then a cup of hot water on a cold day. Now you don't need to run out to the expensive coffee shop. You can make your own!

Ingredients

  • water
  • an adventerous spirit

Steps Microwave

  1. Fill a microwave safe cup, made for hot liquids with water
  2. place the cup, with water into the microwave and shut the door
  3. set the microwave for 30 seconds and press start
  4. once the timer goes off open the microwave and careful stick your finger in the water
  5. if the water is hot take it out and enjoy, if not go back to step 3
