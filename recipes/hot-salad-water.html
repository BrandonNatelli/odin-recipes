Hot Salad Water

A steaming cup of hot salad water

The tradition of drinking hot salad water is deeped rooted in many cultures around the world. This is just one of many recipes passed down throughout the ages.

Ingredients
  • Hot Water
  • dried leaves (tea leaves are a good place to start)
  • sweatener of choice, if desired
Instructions
  1. Follow instructions on how to make hot water
  2. If your dried leaves are already in a bag go ahead and place the bag in the hot water
  3. If the dried leaves are not in a bag, place them in a tea ball, then place in the hot water
  4. Allow the dried leaves to seep in the water for 3-5 minutes, longer if you want a stronger salad water
  5. Now you can remove the tea ball or baged leaves
  6. Sweeten, if dired, to taste
