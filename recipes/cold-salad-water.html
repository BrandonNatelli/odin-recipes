Cold Salad Water

Cold Salad Water, garnished with lemon and lime

Nothing beats an ice cold glass of Sweet(or even unsweet) Salad Water on a hot summer day.

Ingredients
  • Hot Salad Water
  • Sweetener of Choice
  1. Start by making a batch of Hot Salad Water
  2. While still hot add sweeten (if desired) to taste
  3. Chill by placing in the refridgerator or pooring over ice
